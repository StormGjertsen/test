Practical Travel Tips

If you’re looking to visit Scotland it’s important to plan your trip, most importantly pack some good clothing as the weather is unpredictable.

In the bigger cities public transport is accessible, but if you’re in the countryside, renting a car is useful. It’s simple to explore the culture and history of Scotland as many galleries and museums are free. Remember to say “please” and “thank you” as Scottish people value good manners, and are very friendly

Try the local food of Scotland, but don’t forget to also try all the other diverse foods. With some good planning your trip to Scotland will be memorable and fun.
