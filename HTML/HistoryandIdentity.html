A Land of History and Identity

Scotland is a very old country. Throughout the country there are castles, old towns and monuments to remind you of times gone by. Many of these locations reflect tough fights for freedom and the indomitable spirit of the Scottish people.

Scotland’s identity also consists of traditions, including bagpipes, kilts and clan symbols that still have relevance today. There’s history that’s been mixed with old legend, like stories of bold warriors and bizarre creatures. This mix of true stories and good tales is what makes the history of Scotland interesting.

When you learn more about Scotland, you understand why Scottish people are passionate about their country.
